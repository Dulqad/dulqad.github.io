Ingredient

  • Oil
  • Meat

steps for making the dish

  1. Go to market and buy the 20Kg meat
  2. Cut into small pieces
  3. Add spoonful of salt to create a teste
  4. Mix with the oil
  5. Cook for 20-30minutes
  6. put into dish
